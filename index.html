Lawn Care & Water Conservation Tips

Watering Times

The best time to water in Brownsville is between 4 AM and 8 AM to minimize evaporation.

Mowing Height

Keep your grass taller in the summer. Longer grass shades the soil and retains more moisture.

Rain Sensors

Ensure your system is equipped with rain sensors to avoid watering during South Texas rainstorms.
